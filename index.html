About Us

Orewood is a local Suffolk business that specializes in creating handmade wooden goods. We take pride in our craftsmanship and attention to detail, ensuring that each piece we create is unique and of the highest quality.

Our business was founded on a passion for woodworking and a commitment to providing our customers with beautiful, functional pieces that enhance their living spaces. From custom furniture to decorative items, we offer a wide range of products to suit every taste and style.

At Orewood, we believe in the importance of sustainability and environmental responsibility. We source our materials from local suppliers and use eco-friendly finishes to minimize our impact on the environment.

Whether you're looking for a one-of-a-kind piece for your home or a thoughtful gift for a loved one, Orewood is here to help. We invite you to explore our collection and discover the beauty of handmade wooden goods.
